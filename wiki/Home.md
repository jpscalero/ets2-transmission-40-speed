# Welcome to the Antigravity Transmission Wiki

Welcome to the official technical documentation for the **Antigravity 40-Speed Transmission Project**. This mod is not just an accessory; it is a piece of high-precision engineering designed to bridge the gap between standard game physics and extreme engine power.

## 🧠 Engineering Philosophy

### Why 40 Gears?
In Euro Truck Simulator 2, the Prism3D physics engine often struggles with extreme torque values (e.g., engines exceeding 3,000 HP). Standard 12 or 16-speed transmissions create massive "torque jumps" between gears, leading to:
1.  **Physics Instability**: Truck jumping or losing traction.
2.  **Wheel Spin**: Uncontrollable power delivery.
3.  **Low Efficiency**: Inability to stay in the power band.

The **Antigravity 40-Speed** system uses a linear ratio progression (from 15.00 to 0.50) to ensure that the engine's RPM stays within the optimal torque curve, regardless of whether you are pulling 15 tons or 150 tons with 30,000 HP.
