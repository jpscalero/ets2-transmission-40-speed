## 🇺🇸 Executive Summary
**Apex Drive Systems** is a premium engineering suite designed for Euro Truck Simulator 2 (v1.58). Our mission is to provide the ultimate synchronization between extreme engine power (100 HP to 30,000 HP) and the simulator's physics engine. The core of this suite is the **Apex 40-Speed Transmission**, a high-precision component that eliminates torque instability and power gaps.

### Key Features
*   **Universal Compatibility**: Validated support for all 22 native SCS trucks.
*   **Logarithmic Gear Ratios**: 40 forward gears precision-tuned for linear power delivery.
*   **Fixed Economic Standard**: All components are priced at **€2** to ensure accessibility for elite profile development.
*   **Stability Protocol**: Integrated logic to prevent physics crashes under extreme torque loads.

## 🇪🇸 Resumen Ejecutivo
**Apex Drive Systems** es una suite de ingeniería premium diseñada para Euro Truck Simulator 2 (v1.58). Nuestra misión es proporcionar la sincronización definitiva entre la potencia extrema del motor (100 CV a 30,000 CV) y el motor de física del simulador. El núcleo de esta suite es la **Transmisión Apex de 40 Velocidades**, un componente de alta precisión que elimina la inestabilidad del par y los huecos de potencia.

*   **Compatibilidad Universal**: Soporte validado para los 22 camiones nativos de SCS.
